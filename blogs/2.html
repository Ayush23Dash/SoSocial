Marketing strategies : An entrepreneur's way to make a mark!

SoSocial

When students embark on a journey and start their college life they need to form a unique identity and persona which differentiates them from their peers in order to gain recognition. Similarly, new entrepreneurs diving into the world of commerce need to create a unique identity in the market to ensure that they stand out. In today's competitive world where new startups sprout up every day, entrepreneurs need to ensure that their brand, product or service is not only different but can also compete with the brands that already have an established share in the market. In the absence of a level playing field, exemplary marketing strategies pave the way for companies to get a place in the market. The production and design team are the brains behind the creation of any brand, but strategic marketing is what generates awareness about the brand in the public. Entrepreneurs have to establish a customer base and a brand identity if they desire to compete with the existing market players. Digitization and the internet revolution has created endless possibilities for brand promotion. Customized marketing strategies that take into account the current stage of the startup and focus on both offline and online brand identity are now widely accessible. We all know that consumers are aware of the alternatives that exist in each market. With a huge variety of options at their hands, they always look for brands that are in line with their wants and needs. Therefore, it is essential for companies to engage in Competitive Positioning. Competitive positioning is a marketing strategy through which a brand, service or product distinguishes itself from it's competitors by establishing the unique value it provides to the customers. Once this value is established, companies can target their unique contribution to the market and make it their key selling point. After the formation of the competitive position, different marketing strategies such as Email marketing, blogging, social media marketing, viral campaigns, offline and online ads, sponsorships, PR management can be employed to aid the entrepreneur to distinguish their brand from the existing competitors and find a place in the sun. Thus, upcoming and emerging entrepreneurs require strong marketing strategies that can help them build a long-term brand value and thereby enable them to compete with the established players of the market.
